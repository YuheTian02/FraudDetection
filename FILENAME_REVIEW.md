# Filename Review and Recommendations

## Current Filenames Analysis

### Issues Found:
1. **Folder name**: `FraudDetection` - Could be more descriptive
2. **Spaces in filenames**: `Fraud Detection Case Study.pdf` - Should use underscores/hyphens
3. **Inconsistent casing**: `FraudCaseStudy.xlsx` vs `p2_dataset.xlsx`
4. **Unclear names**: `p2_dataset.xlsx`, `Entire_Work.ipynb`
5. **Redundant files**: Multiple notebook versions

## Professional Naming Standards

### Best Practices:
- ✅ Use lowercase with underscores or hyphens
- ✅ Be descriptive and clear
- ✅ Avoid spaces (use underscores or hyphens)
- ✅ Use consistent naming patterns
- ✅ Keep concise but informative
